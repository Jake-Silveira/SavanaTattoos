The Master at Work

Hey everyone! 👋
My name is Savana✨. I was born in Texas and raised in Illinois, but I’ve been back in Texas for 8 years now!
I’ve been tattooing since 2022. I’m a mix of self-taught and apprenticed. I love doing illustrative, fine line, and botanical styles. I’m excited to take on more styles in the future!
When I’m not tattooing, I enjoy acrylic painting, cooking, nature, true crime, and trying new things. 🌱🫶🏻
